Policy

Last Updated: April 21, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, upload a resume, or post a job opening. This may include your name, email address, professional history, and skills.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to facilitate the matching process between candidates and employers, to personalize your experience, and to improve our AI algorithms.

3. Data Sharing

We share candidate profiles with potential employers as part of the matching process. We do not sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Choices

You can update your profile information or delete your account at any time through your account settings.

6. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze site traffic. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.
